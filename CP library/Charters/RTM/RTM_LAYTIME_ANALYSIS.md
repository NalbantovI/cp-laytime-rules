(a) Charterer shall pay to Owner demurrage at the rates specified in the Fixture Note; per day of

18.1 All time and expenditure relating to the covering and uncovering of hatches shall be for Owner's

18.3 If weather is inclement or wet the Master shall have all hatches closed when loading or discharging has
finished for the day.

loading or discharging is not in progress.

Overtime expenses are to be paid by the party ordering same, except for overtime expenses for the
